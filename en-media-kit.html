Media kit · commercial transparency

Partnerships compatible with independence

Limited formats, visible disclosure and complete separation between funding, data, methodology and conclusions.

No invented metrics

Audience figures will be supplied with date, period and source once a stable sample exists.

Scientific brief sponsorship

Disclosed presence outside the conclusion and data. Sponsors do not review or approve the brief.

Library sponsorship

Visible association with a theme — oceanography, ports or geology — without presenting commercial links as sources.

Commissioned technical report

Only on compatible topics. Funding, method and potential conflicts are disclosed.

Programmatic advertising

It will not be enabled until traffic is stable, consent is appropriate and the mobile experience remains clean.

What is not for sale
  • Scientific conclusions and seismic interpretation.
  • Removal of verified information.
  • Undisclosed advertising or commercial links presented as sources.
  • Readers’ personal data.
Request information

Independent educational project. Automated data do not replace civil-protection or professional navigation information.
