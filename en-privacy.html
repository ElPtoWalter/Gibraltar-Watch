TRANSPARENCY

Privacy

The site works without accounts, forms or first-party analytics. External services activate only when needed.

Controller and contact

Gibraltar Watch is an independent educational project. For privacy or content enquiries, write to contacto@estrechogibraltar.com.

Browsing without accounts or forms

The site does not require registration, create user profiles or operate first-party forms. This version installs no first-party analytics or advertising cookies.

When you email us

If you contact us voluntarily, we process the sending address, any name you include, the message, attachments and the technical metadata needed to manage the conversation. The purpose is to reply, review corrections or sources and handle potential collaborations. Messages are not added to advertising lists.

Email routing and providers

Messages sent to the project address are forwarded through Cloudflare Email Routing to a verified destination mailbox. Cloudflare and the mailbox provider may process technical information under their own terms and policies.

Retention

Conversations are kept for the period reasonably needed to answer and preserve the context of the request. They may be retained longer when necessary for security, legal claims or applicable obligations.

Rights and requests

You may request access, correction, deletion, objection or restriction concerning personal data included in a conversation by writing to contacto@estrechogibraltar.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency.

External services

Loading the AIS radar connects your browser to MarineTraffic. The seismic map uses OpenStreetMap/Carto tiles and Leaflet. These providers may receive standard technical information such as IP address, browser and referrer.

Hosting and scientific data

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may record technical access information under its policy. Published JSON files contain scientific data and public sources, not personal visitor profiles.

Future advertising or analytics

Before adding advertising or analytics that require consent, an appropriate mechanism will be introduced for European Economic Area visitors and this policy will be updated.

Independent educational project. Automated seismic data do not replace civil-protection information or professional navigation services.
