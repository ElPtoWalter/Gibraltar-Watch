Activity does not equal closure

Earthquakes show stress release across a diffuse plate boundary. Small-event frequency can change due to detection improvements, catalogue revisions or local sequences.

  • This dashboard does not predict earthquakes.
  • It does not claim a baseline without a long homogeneous series.
