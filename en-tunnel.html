WHAT IS BEING DONE

Campaigns to reduce uncertainty

The SECEGSA–CSIC agreement published in late 2025 funds 2025–2026 work to investigate the seabed and improve the 3D geological model, with IGME, IEO, ICM, IHM and USGS participation.

  • Bathymetry and geomorphology.
  • Nature of shallow geological formations.
  • Campaign design and execution around the Camarinal Sill.
  • Physical-environment data management system.
WHAT IS NOT CONFIRMED

No construction timetable

There is no construction award, official start date or opening date. Cost or inauguration estimates outside official sources should be treated as hypotheses.
